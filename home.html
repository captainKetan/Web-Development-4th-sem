Semi-Conductor Laboratory (SCL) strives on the policy to manage its activities in a responsible manner as to avoid causing any risks to the health and safety of its personnel. With the technology upgradation and rapid expansion of newer processes, self-help in minimising the occurrence/devastating effects of accidents has become essential for all industries.

Wafer processing and its allied operations at SCL require handling of large number of hazardous gases and chemicals. Various gases/ chemicals are pyrophoric, toxic, corrosive or flammable in nature. To ensure incident free operations at SCL, the installed safety systems and their up-keep thus is of great importance. Different safety installations, comprising of fire protection and detection system, Toxic Gas Monitoring (TGM) system, Chemical Leak Detection system, etc. are maintained for 100% up-time on 24x7 basis.

Fire & Safety Section (FSS) is entrusted with the responsibilities to ensure overall safety of operations at SCL. Main functions of FSS involves:
  • Up-keep of various safety installations viz. Fire Hydrant and Sprinkler System, Fire Detection system, Toxic Gas Monitoring system, Chemical Leak Detection system etc.
  • Attending emergency calls.
  • Planning for ERT functions.
  • Conducting safety mock drill.
  • Conducting safety training programmes for SCL staff/ contractor's labour at site.
